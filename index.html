About Us

Who Am I?

Hi there, welcome to my digital portfolio. I'm Lucas.I am a young keen worker and Design graduate from Goldsmiths University, London. I have a wide skill-set and a strong will to learn new practices. The majority of my professional experience is in hospitality, service, design, gardening and construction. Personally, I have experience in a wide range of digital working practices like videography, music production, graphics and 3D modelling.

I am an experienced crafts worker in metalworking, sculpting and casting and have a great passion for jewelry making, prop making, constructing tools and works of art. I am looking for a position as an intern in design studios / companies (big or small, hand craft or digital etc.), construction firms, or environmentally oriented organizations as this most interests me and keeps me motivated. Am open to roles in other areas depending on the position.
